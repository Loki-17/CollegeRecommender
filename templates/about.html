About College Recommender

Our mission is to help students find the perfect college match based on their preferences and needs.

College Recommender uses advanced AI and machine learning techniques to analyze your preferences and match them with our extensive database of Indian colleges. We process thousands of student reviews and college profiles to deliver personalized recommendations.

How Our Recommendation System Works

The Science Behind Our Recommendations

Our college recommendation system uses a linear regression model combined with natural language processing to analyze your preferences. Here's how it works:

  1. Data Collection: We gather data from thousands of student reviews across Indian colleges.
  2. Text Processing: We clean and process this text data to extract meaningful features.
  3. Feature Extraction: Our system identifies important keywords and patterns in college reviews.
  4. Linear Regression Model: We use linear regression to predict how well each college matches your preferences.
  5. Ranking: Colleges are ranked based on their predicted match score with your input.
